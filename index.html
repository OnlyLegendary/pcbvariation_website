PCB Variation is one project, many board fingerprints

This site is all about my PCB Variation project: assembly bounty + temporal physical fingerprinting. The idea is to prove a board’s identity from how it’s built and how it behaves over time, not just from a label. It’s hands-on and messy, and I’m documenting the real process here.
